Feature Deep Dive

🗂️ Group Tabs by Domain

What it does: Instantly groups all open tabs based on domain (e.g., YouTube, GitHub, etc.).

Why it matters: Reduces clutter and keeps similar tabs neatly together.

How to use: Open the extension and click “Group Tabs”.

🧠 Smart Subgrouping (Pro)

What it does: Groups tabs not just by domain, but also by URL path (e.g., different Amazon product pages go into different subgroups).

Why it matters: Gives power users precision-level organization within the same site.

How to use: Upgrade to Pro → Enable “Path-based Subgrouping” in settings → Click “Group Tabs”.

Pro Benefit: Deep tab clarity. Better than Chrome’s native grouping.

🔍 Smart Tab Search

What it does: Instantly searches across all your tabs.

Why it matters: No more hunting or tab chaos — just type and go.

How to use: Open EazyNet → Use the search bar to filter tabs by keyword.

⚙️ Auto Tab Grouping (Pro)

What it does: Automatically groups new tabs by domain or path without manual action.

Why it matters: Fully automated — ideal for multitaskers and researchers.

How to use: Enable “Auto Grouping” from the extension settings (Pro only).

Pro Benefit: Hands-free productivity boost.

✏️ Mapping Updates

What it does: Allows renaming or re-mapping groups per domain (e.g., “gmail.com” = Work).

Why it matters: Personalized tab logic for your workflow.

How to use: Click the ✏️ icon next to a group → Save your mapping.

Free: 5 updates / domain • Pro: Unlimited

🛌 Tab Memory Saver

What it does: Snoozes unused tabs to free up memory.

Why it matters: Keeps Chrome fast without closing tabs.

How to use: In the tab search view → Click “Snooze” on a tab → “Restore” when needed.

Free: 10 snoozed tabs • Pro: Unlimited

🧊 Group Memory Saver (Pro)

What it does: Snoozes all tabs in a group with one click.

Why it matters: Efficient way to pause an entire project/workstream.

How to use: In the “All Groups” section → Click “Snooze Group”.

Pro Benefit: Maximize memory savings instantly.

🔁 Auto Memory Management (Pro)

What it does: Automatically snoozes unused tabs every 30 mins.

Why it matters: Saves RAM without thinking about it.

How to use: Go to extension settings → Enable “Auto Memory Management”.

Pro Benefit: Set it and forget it — your browser stays light.

🌐 Sync Across Devices (Pro)

What it does: Keeps your groups and settings synced across all Chrome devices.

Why it matters: Seamless transitions between work and home setups.

How to use: Just sign into Chrome on all devices with sync enabled.

Pro Benefit: Truly portable tab management.

📩 Priority Support (Pro)

What it does: Pro users get faster, personalized help.

Why it matters: Get answers and fixes quickly.

How to use: Email eazynettabmanager@gmail.com with “Pro Support” in the subject.
